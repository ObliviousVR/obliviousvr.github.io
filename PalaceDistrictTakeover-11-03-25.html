Palace District Under Hemwick Agency Control: A New Era of Reform or Tyranny?

ZAPHIRA, PALACE DISTRICT – The Palace District is in a state of upheaval following the Hemwick Agency's hostile takeover. While the agency promises a new era of prosperity and reform, critics decry the move as a tyrannical power grab.

The Lower Zaphiran council has officially endorsed the takeover, citing the need to restore order to the long-neglected district. However, this decision has been met with fierce resistance from residents and palace district businesses who view it as a betrayal.

"The council's decision is a betrayal of the people," decried a displaced palace district citizen. "They've traded our freedom for a mercenary's boot on our necks."

Hemwick Agency mercenaries and undead operatives have established a heavy presence throughout the district, enforcing strict curfews and prohibiting public gatherings. Protests have been met with deadly force, and reports of civilian casualties continue to surface.

Despite the violence, the Hemwick Agency insists that its presence will bring much-needed improvements to the Palace District. They have promised economic stability, debt recovery plans, and massive investments in education and healthcare.

"We are committed to restoring order and ensuring the safety of all residents," stated Councilman Hemwick. "Any resistance will be met with necessary force."

Councilman Hemwick has also laid out a series of political stances, including:

  • Religious, sexual, and gender freedom
  • Encouragement of the arts, education, and mental health
  • Discouragement of vices and humanoid slavery
  • Plans for future reforms

The sudden shift in power has left many residents in a state of shock and fear. Businesses are shuttered, and the once vibrant atmosphere of the Palace District has been replaced by a chilling silence punctuated by the sound of gunfire and the whir of surveillance drones.

The international community has yet to issue a formal response. Some observers suggest that the Hemwick Agency's move may have been long pre-meditated and done with support from those within the council's inner circles.

The future of the Palace District hangs in the balance. Whether Hemwick’s regime can maintain control, or whether the simmering resentment will erupt into full-scale rebellion, remains to be seen.
